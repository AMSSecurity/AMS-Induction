AMS Policies & Procedures

AMS Security Policies and Procedures are updated as needed by law, or every two years. All employees must read, understand, and sign all policies annually or upon any changes.

Each work site requires a site induction via the Guardhouse App — annually or upon any updates — completed alongside the OP19A Site Induction Form. A copy should be in your employment pack.

Note: Full policy documents are available via the Guardhouse App. Some work sites may also have a physical Policy & Procedure folder — however, please be aware that paper copies may not reflect the latest version of each policy. Always refer to the Guardhouse App for the most up-to-date documents.

04 — Compliance

Reporting

🚨 When to Complete a Security Incident Report (SIR)

AMS SIR is mandatory for all of the following:

✅ Assaults (especially on security staff)
✅ Serious disturbances or crimes
✅ Fire alarms / evacuations
✅ First aid incidents (slips, trips, falls)
✅ Thefts (≥ ~$50 or officer attended)
✅ Lost or found valuables
✅ All property damage / vandalism
✅ Security system alarm activations
✅ Banned offender breaches / returns
✅ Motor vehicle accidents
✅ Syringe finds (CODE RAINBOW)
✅ Significant police / emergency attendances
✏️ Log Entry Only — No SIR Required
  • Very minor thefts (no security attendance)
  • Minor alcohol-related matters (complied and left, no disturbance)
  • Some minor disturbances — if unsure, check with AMS management per OP003
🛒 Suspected Shoplifter

Refer to OP004 Retail Theft Procedure. Complete an SIR if value > ~$50 and a security officer attended or was involved at the time.

📍 Site-Specific Requirements
  • Some sites have their own forms in addition to the AMS SIR
  • Requirements vary per site — covered during your site induction
  • Check with Centre Manager or onsite supervisor if unsure
📋 General Reporting Rules
  • Log everything — every incident goes in the Security Log / Patrol Journal, including SIR reference numbers
  • Carry a notebook — record contemporaneous notes; admissible to refresh memory in court

In essence: Log everything. Complete the mandatory AMS Shift Report every shift and an AMS SIR for anything serious, injurious, criminal, or involving valuables. Complete any site-specific forms as outlined during induction. Escalate critical events immediately per OP002 & OP003. Proofread carefully and stay professional at all times.
Related policies: OP002 Site Contacts · OP003 Communications & Escalation · OP004 Retail Theft · OP005 Banned Offender · HR012 Lost Property
